About Us

The Errno Computer Technologies Club, based at the Faculty of Physics and Astronomy of Adam Mickiewicz University in Poznań, is dedicated to fostering a passion for computer technologies among students. We organize workshops, talks, and projects covering various IT disciplines. Our goal is to create a collaborative environment where members can explore their interests and develop their skills.

Our Projects

SNS MINERWA

Minerwa is a virtual shooting sports training system designed for high schools and elementary schools. Easy and safe way to learn/teach how to shoot a gun.

LAB VR

It's an innovative classroom simulation where physics is an adventure, geology becomes an expedition, astronomy turns into a journey among the stars, and computer technology devices come to life in fascinating ways!

PLANETARIUM AR

Planetarium AR is an innovative application designed to bring the wonders of the cosmos directly to your fingertips through the power of augmented reality technology. Users can embark on a journey through the Solar System.
